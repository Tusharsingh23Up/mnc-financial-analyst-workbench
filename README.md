# MNC Financial Analyst Workbench

An end-to-end Financial Analyst portfolio project built to demonstrate practical finance, valuation, business analytics, and dashboard reporting skills required for Financial Analyst, FP&A Analyst, Finance Data Analyst, Business Analyst, and Valuation Analyst roles.

This project combines financial statement analysis, 3-statement modeling, DCF valuation, peer benchmarking, scenario analysis, and browser-based executive dashboard reporting using sample Big Tech financial data.

---

## Project Overview

The goal of this project is to simulate how a Financial Analyst analyzes company performance and converts raw financial data into business insights.

The project focuses on:

- Financial statement analysis
- Revenue and profitability analysis
- Ratio analysis
- 3-statement financial modeling
- Free cash flow forecasting
- DCF valuation
- Peer benchmarking
- Scenario analysis
- KPI dashboard reporting
- Analyst-style management memo

This project is designed as a portfolio-ready case study for MNC finance and analytics roles.

---

## Business Problem

Companies and investors need to understand whether a business is financially strong, efficiently managed, fairly valued, and capable of future growth.

This project answers key business questions such as:

- How has the company performed over the past few years?
- Is revenue growing consistently?
- Are margins improving or declining?
- How strong is the company’s cash flow generation?
- How does the company compare with peers?
- What is the estimated intrinsic value using DCF valuation?
- What are the key risks and business recommendations?

The sample project uses Big Tech companies for demonstration:

## Project Features

### 1. Financial Statement Analysis

Analyzed income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement data to understand company performance.

Key areas covered:

- Revenue
- Gross profit
- EBIT
- Net income
- Total assets
- Total liabilities
- Cash flow from operations
- Capital expenditure
- Free cash flow

---

### 2. Ratio Analysis

Calculated important financial ratios used by analysts and finance teams.

Ratios included:

- Revenue CAGR
- Gross margin
- EBIT margin
- Net profit margin
- Free cash flow margin
- Return on equity
- Return on invested capital
- Debt-to-equity ratio
- Current ratio
- Interest coverage ratio

---

### 3. 3-Statement Financial Modeling

Built a financial modeling structure covering:

- Historical financials
- Forecast assumptions
- Revenue forecast
- Expense forecast
- Profitability forecast
- Working capital assumptions
- Free cash flow forecast
- Valuation assumptions

---

### 4. DCF Valuation

Performed discounted cash flow valuation using:

- Free cash flow forecast
- Discount rate assumption
- Terminal growth assumption
- Exit multiple approach
- Terminal value
- Present value of cash flows
- Equity value
- Equity value per share

---

### 5. Peer Benchmarking

Compared companies across important business and financial metrics.

Benchmarking areas:

- Revenue growth
- EBIT margin
- Net margin
- Free cash flow generation
- ROIC
- Leverage
- Liquidity
- Valuation multiples

---

### 6. Scenario Analysis

Created multiple valuation scenarios:

- Base case
- Bull case
- Bear case

This helps understand how changes in growth, margin, discount rate, and exit multiple impact valuation.

---

### 7. Dashboard Reporting

Created a browser-based finance analytics dashboard to present insights in a professional format.

Dashboard includes:

- KPI cards
- Company selector
- Scenario selector
- Revenue analysis
- Margin analysis
- DCF valuation output
- Peer comparison
- Analyst summary
